About

I am an experienced technical content developer with 10 years of experience, developing technical content such as hardware installation guides, SaaS product guides, quick start guides, user manuals, video scripts, REST API guides, and GraphQL guides for large tech companies.

My area of expertise is language and writing. Combining my love of writing and technology, technical writing is a career path that I'm passionate about. I specialize in crafting customer-facing documentation that helps users get started quickly and succeed.

My personal strength is building relationships. As a technical writer, I regularly meet with stakeholders and team members to get information. I take the time to build connections, which helps me get information from a wider variety of sources and makes my documentation more complete.

Outside of work, I'm a lifelong learner with a love of languages. I speak Japanese and German, and I'm always looking for ways to keep improving. When I'm not studying, you can usually find me working on a textile project, whether that's cross stitch, crochet, or knitting. I find that working with my hands is a great way to recharge after a day of working with words. I'm also a devoted cat person, and my cats are happy to supervise all of the above.
